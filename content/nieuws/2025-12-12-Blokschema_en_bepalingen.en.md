We're ending the year with some practical information: the block schedule for the Head of the River Amstel 2026 has been released! Starting today, you can find it on our block schedule page, giving everyone an early look at the approximate timing of their event.   
The final start times for each event will follow after the draw and, as usual, will be published in the days leading up to the race.

But that’s not all. The Rules and Regulations for 2026 have also been updated—useful for anyone wanting to review the latest guidelines, safety information, and practical details surrounding the race.

Here’s to a great Head 2026!
