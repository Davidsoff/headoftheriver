---
title: "Is it allowed to start in a masters field with one or more pre-masters on board?"
question: "Is it allowed to start in a masters field with one or more pre-masters on board?"
weight: 6
---

Yes, it is allowed to start in a masters field with one or more pre-masters on board. The requirements for the average age do not change as a result. The average age of all rowers in the crew determines which field may be started. For example, the average age for masters C must be at least 43.
