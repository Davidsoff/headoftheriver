On the menu: good coffee, homemade pastries, delicious sandwiches and matcha for the enthusiast. The vibe? Cheerful, warm, just right. Portuguese tiles, dark wood and a solid lick of beige. Plop down with a bica – espresso in Portuguese (aha!) – and a piece of banana bread. Or grab a coffee to go for your walk along the Weesperzijde.
