The former "Directeurswoning" of the Zuidergasfabriek was restored in 2013 and opened to the public in 2014, in a new guise: culinary and artistic café tHuis aan de Amstel. Authentic, creative, local, pure, accessible, and above all: just good food and drink. At any time of the day and evening, tHuis aan de Amstel offers a relaxed homely setting.
